Public Institutions

University of Kwazulu-Natal
The University of KwaZulu-Natal (UKZN) is a prominent public research university located in South Africa, with multiple campuses in and around Durban. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines, including arts, sciences, engineering, health sciences, and business.
Durban University of Technology
The Durban University of Technology (DUT) is a public university in South Africa, located in Durban. It offers a range of programs across various fields, including engineering, health sciences, arts, and business. DUT is known for its focus on practical and applied learning, as well as its strong emphasis on research and community engagement.
University of Zululand
The University of Zululand (UniZulu) is a public university located in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. It offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various fields, including arts, sciences, education, commerce, and law.
Mangosuthu University of Technology
The Mangosuthu University of Technology (MUT) is a public university located in Umlazi, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. It focuses on providing higher education in engineering, natural sciences, and management sciences, among other fields.
